Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2024

1. Introduction

ScoutLens ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Service.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Email Address: If you subscribe to our newsletter, we collect your email address. This is optional.
  • Payment Information: If you purchase a Pro subscription, payment is processed by Stripe or PayPal. We do not store your payment details.

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: We use Vercel Analytics to collect anonymous usage statistics (page views, device type, etc.)
  • Local Storage: We store your watchlist, preferences, and settings locally on your device using browser localStorage
  • Cookies: We may use cookies for analytics and functionality. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Send you weekly newsletters (if you subscribed)
  • Improve our Service based on usage patterns
  • Process Pro subscription payments
  • Respond to your inquiries

4. Data Storage

Local Storage: Your watchlist and preferences are stored locally on your device. We never send this data to our servers.

Email Addresses: If you subscribe, your email is stored securely. We use this only to send you newsletters.

Analytics: Usage data is collected anonymously by Vercel Analytics and cannot identify you personally.

5. Data Sharing

We do NOT:

  • Sell your data to third parties
  • Share your email with advertisers
  • Track you across other websites
  • Use your data for purposes other than stated above

We may share:

  • Anonymous usage statistics with analytics providers (Vercel)
  • Payment information with payment processors (Stripe, PayPal) - they handle this securely

6. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Vercel Analytics: Anonymous usage tracking (privacy-friendly)
  • Stripe/PayPal: Payment processing (they handle your payment data securely)
  • Newsletter Service: If you subscribe, your email may be stored by our newsletter provider

These services have their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing them.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request what data we have about you
  • Delete: Request deletion of your email from our newsletter list
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from newsletters at any time
  • Local Data: Clear your browser's localStorage to remove locally stored data

To exercise these rights, contact us through our website or social media.

8. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • Secure payment processing through Stripe/PayPal
  • Local storage (your watchlist never leaves your device)

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us.

10. International Users

If you are using our Service from outside the jurisdiction where our servers are located, your data may be transferred to and stored in that jurisdiction. By using our Service, you consent to this transfer.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

12. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our website or social media.

By using ScoutLens, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy.
